Facts About US

The company is established as the leading specialist in purchase and recycling of various wastes, mainly Electronics/IT Equipment and we accomplished this by providing manageable solutions and locating profitable end-markets for obsolete and recyclable materials. In recent years, these services have been refined to focus on the asset recovery value of electronics devices and electronics components such as mobile phones, laptops computers, decoders, Internet modems/ router, electronic devices, telecom equipment and other affiliated items.
