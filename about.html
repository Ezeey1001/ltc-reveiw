About LTC-AFRICA

LTC AFRICA LIMITED is registered Liability Company which Headquarter is located at: Osu Accra, Ghana, with warehouse Facilities in Lagos- Nigeria, Dar es Salaam -Tanzania, Windhoek- Namibia and Antananarivo -Madagascar. Started it operation in March 2010 in Ghana, which has grown into a truly known organization across Africa, with representatives and partners across the African States. Having more than 28 Direct and 80 In-Direct employees.

The company is established as the leading specialist in purchase and recycling of various wastes, mainly Electronics/IT Equipment and we accomplished this by providing manageable solutions and locating profitable end-markets for obsolete and recyclable materials. In recent years, these services have been refined to focus on the asset recovery value of electronics devices and electronics components such as mobile phones, laptops computers, decoders, Internet modems/ router, electronic devices, telecom equipment and other affiliated item

The Company is one of the largest buyers of liquidated IT equipment asset across Africa from both telecoms operators and service provider in large volume, our growth is dedicated both by expanding opportunities and also our commitment to provide reliable and most dynamic recycling solution
